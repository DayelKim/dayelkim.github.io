HIGH HAZARD AREA MAGLOCK & SAFETY MAT

Purpose

To ensure the safety of operators by implementing a system that stops and interlocks VFDs

Operation

If Unlock push button is pushed, then safety relay will be open and VFDs will come to a complete stop in 10 seconds. Once machine is stopped, then mag lock door will open and VFDs will be interlocked. While standing on safety mat door can not be locked. Once operator exit the hazard area, close door and pull reset button, system is ready to start up again.
